Our Story

The Vision of Paradise

Our Founding Story

Paradise Retreat was founded in 2010 by the visionary travel enthusiast, Clara Hayes. Her dream was to create a sanctuary where luxury meets nature, a place where the stress of the modern world melts away with the tide. It started as a single, exclusive villa and has grown into the world-class resort it is today.

Hospitality Mission

Our mission is simple: **To deliver an unparalleled, personalized experience for every guest.** We believe true luxury is found in impeccable service, attention to detail, and a genuine, warm welcome. We are committed to sustainability, ensuring our paradise remains beautiful for generations to come.

Our Milestones

2010: Foundation

The first Private Villa opens its doors, establishing a new standard for tropical luxury.

2015: Expansion

Launch of the 5-star fine dining restaurant, "The Coral Kitchen," and the Infinity Pool.

2018: Award-Winning

Voted 'Best Tropical Resort' by Global Traveler Magazine for the first of many times.

2022: Sustainability

Achieved a 'Green Key' certification for our commitment to eco-friendly operations.
